The House

A Paris atelier with a quietly defiant point of view.

We've been making clothes for nine years. Long enough to have opinions; not long enough to be sentimental about them.

N°01 — Material

Cloth above novelty.

We work only with mills we've visited. If the material isn't honest, the design can't save it. Our wool comes from Biella, our silk from Como, our linen from Roubaix.

N°02 — Cut

Patterns drafted on bodies.

Every pattern is fitted on at least nine different women before production. The clothes are designed to move, sit, breathe, and disappear into your day.

N°03 — Time

Slow is the only fast that lasts.

We release two collections a year, both small. Stock is finite. When a piece is gone, it's gone — until we feel like making it again.
